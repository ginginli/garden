Secret Hunting Tips

1

Use Shift Lock

Enable shift lock to look around corners and through walls. Essential for finding hidden entrances and glitching into secret areas.

2

Try Emotes

Use /e dance2 and other emotes near walls and corners. Some secret areas require emote glitching to access.

3

Explore Hills

The hill behind the Seed Shop has multiple secrets. Climb to the top and explore every corner thoroughly.

Some walls can be walked through! Try walking into suspicious-looking walls, especially near shops and plot entrances.
